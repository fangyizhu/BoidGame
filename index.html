Eat Them All

A Game based on boids. Cross platform runnable application is in the repository.

Goal:

Eat all the agents in the game.

Normals (white) :

They try to run away from you, and can cross the edges.

Infected Killers (red) :

They try to kill you. Eat one the game ends. Can not cross the edges. They don't follow other agents and they don't avoid you. Start to chase you when get too close. Dangerous species.

Speed Risers (green) :

Increase your speed for a little. Can not cross the edges. Try to run away from you.

Killer Purifiers () :

Turn 4 killers to normals. If there are less than four killers on the map. Their effect lasts until new killers occur. Can not cross the edges. Try to run away from you.
